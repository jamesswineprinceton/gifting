Friday, February 23, 2024 at 19:00

New York, NY


My friend Gray—a true southerner who had never visited the city—found this spot that me, a seasoned traveler to New York, had never heard of. How lucky am I to have someone who is willing to find new things like these! Little Island remains one of my favorite places to watch the sunset in New York. I find joy in the orange glow projected upon the towers of FiDi, and introspection in the setting sun beyond the Jersey City shore.
