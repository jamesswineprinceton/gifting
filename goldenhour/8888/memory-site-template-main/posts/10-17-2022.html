Columbus, GA


There's something about the humidity, the heat, the latitude that makes the Georgia sky a canvas every evening. I don't know why, but the sunsets there will always be the prettiest I have ever seen. Nowhere else can produce the same clouds, the same colors, the same backdrops as these evenings in the Deep South.
