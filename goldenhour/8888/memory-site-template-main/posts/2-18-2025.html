Golden Hour

Tuesday, February 18, 2025 at 17:37

Princeton, NJ


As the silhouette of the Grad College peaks out from behind the peaks of dead Oaks, I try to imagine a visitor's perspective of Princeton. It's a feeling I've forgotten. How strange is it to have a hamlet formed of castle buildings hidden in the woods of a state that, at many times, feels like one continuous strip mall? How odd must you feel to go from the wastelands of Elizabeth to the mysterious, shrouded Gothic spires of this place?

Princeton, Lake Carnegie




By James Swinehart
